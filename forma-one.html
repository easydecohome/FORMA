Straight answers

Before you ask.

Is it really only $3,490? What's the catch?
The catch is that you assemble it. That's it. There's no showroom, no salesperson and no installer in the price — that's the $10,000+ a renovation company adds on top of cabinets like these. The photos on this page are the actual product, benchtop and sink included.
Can I actually build this myself?
If you've ever assembled flat-pack furniture, yes. Panels arrive pre-drilled and labelled in build order with a full-size plan. Most owners finish in a weekend with a drill, a level and a second pair of hands for the overheads. Stuck? Call us — a human who has built this exact kitchen answers.
What if my space is a different size?
FORMA ONE is a fixed layout — that's how it stays at $3,490. If your walls need something different, FORMA CUSTOM is the same system cut to your exact measurements, from $760 per linear metre.
What if something arrives damaged or is missing?
Photograph it and send it through. Replacement parts ship free and jump the queue, and we confirm your replacement timeline within 24 hours. Transit damage is 100% our problem, not yours.
Can I see it in person first?
There's no showroom — that's a big part of why it's $3,490 instead of $15,000. What we can do: a live video call walking around an installed kitchen, more photos of any detail you want, and reviews from owners who've built one.
What's the warranty?
10 years on cabinetry and supplied hardware under normal domestic use — delamination, board failure, hinge and runner mechanisms. Assembly mishaps aren't covered, but replacement panels are cheap and we'll talk you through any fix.
