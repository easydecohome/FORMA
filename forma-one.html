Why is it so cheap?
It isn't cheap — it's direct. You're paying for the kitchen without a showroom, a salesperson, a project manager and an installer stacked on top of it. Those people are most of a $20,000 quote. Where is it made?
It's built to our specification in our partner factory overseas — the same way most kitchens sold in Australia are, including plenty of the $20,000 ones. The difference is we buy direct at factory pricing and pass that on, instead of routing it through a distributor and a retailer. It's quality-checked before it goes out, and the 10-year warranty is ours, honoured here in Australia. If something fails, you deal with us, not an exporter.
